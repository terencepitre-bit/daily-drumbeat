NAACP Launches $20M Get-Out-The-Vote Drive at Chicago Convention

VOTING RIGHTS — The NAACP convention in Chicago, themed "We the People," is launching a $20 million get-out-the-vote campaign ahead of midterm elections. The 117th NAACP National Convention is taking place at McCormick Place in Chicago, focusing on civic engagement, voting rights, and encouraging the next generation of voters. The theme is intended to remind that the people have the power, and in America there is no peace.

The takeaway
The NAACP's unprecedented $20 million mobilization signals an emergency-level response to threats against Black voting rights and political power.
